# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful, constructive and welcoming for contributors of different experience levels and backgrounds.

## Expected behaviour

- Discuss ideas and code, not personal characteristics.
- Give specific, actionable and technically grounded feedback.
- Assume good intent while remaining clear about correctness and reproducibility.
- Credit sources, datasets and prior work.
- Protect private, confidential and personally identifiable information.
- Help learners understand why a change is needed rather than only stating that it is wrong.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, deliberate misinformation, plagiarism, disclosure of private information and repeated disruptive conduct are not acceptable.

## Scope

This code applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews and other project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer. Reports will be reviewed in context and may result in warnings, content removal, temporary restrictions or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

## Attribution

This policy is inspired by common open-source community standards and is intentionally written in concise project-specific language.
